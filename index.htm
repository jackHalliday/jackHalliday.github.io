Biography

I’m a post doctoral researcher working on the MAGPIE pulsed-power generator. My research interests include: X-Ray driven plasma ablation experiments, laboratory astrophysics, and plasma diagnostic development.

The diagnostics with which I work most frequently are: Optical Thomson scattering, X-Ray self emission imaging, and X-Ray continuum spectroscopy.
